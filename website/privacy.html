PinkRain Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19 May 2025

1. Data Collection

PinkRain does not collect or store any personal information. Everything you enter—like medication schedules, mood logs, or notes—remains locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to DoublOne Studios or any third parties.

2. Permissions Used

PinkRain requests only what it needs to function:

  • Notifications – to remind you about medication and mood check‑ins. All notifications are generated and delivered on‑device; no external push services (e.g., FCM) are used.

3. Analytics & Tracking

PinkRain contains no analytics frameworks and performs no remote tracking. Usage data never leaves your phone.

4. Data Security

Because your data never leaves your device, you maintain complete control. PinkRain does not create external backups, and DoublOne Studios cannot access or restore your information.

5. Children’s Privacy

PinkRain is intended for adults aged 18 and over. It is not designed for, nor marketed to, children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If substantial changes are made, we will update this page and adjust the effective date above. PinkRain does not operate user accounts, so you will not receive in‑app change notices.
